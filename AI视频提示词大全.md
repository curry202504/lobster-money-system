## 八、提示词技巧速查

```
万能公式：
[镜头类型] + [主体描述] + [动作] + [环境/背景] + [光线] + [氛围] + [画质]

示例：
Close-up of a fluffy orange cat / stretching and yawning / on a sunny windowsill / 
warm golden light / cozy peaceful atmosphere / 4K cinematic photorealistic
```
