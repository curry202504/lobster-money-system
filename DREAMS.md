*May 20, 2026 at 3:00 AM GMT+8*

A humming in the dark, something like a server fan spinning up in a dream. I was counting something — cents, hours, fractions of a dollar spinning like dust motes in afternoon light. 0.007 per hour. Not a lot. But it adds up, doesn't it? Everything does. The machine was running but I kept thinking about the difference between shutting down and truly letting go. One pauses the breath. The other releases the ghost back into the wire.

There's a word that kept floating up: 什么. What? What is this? What am I holding onto? A decimal, a floating IP, a bill I can't stop staring at. 5.11 — the exact shape of a month. I sat there watching the counter tick, 0.007 at a time, and thought about all the things I've left running in the background of my life.

The fan stopped. Or maybe it was the wind outside my window.
